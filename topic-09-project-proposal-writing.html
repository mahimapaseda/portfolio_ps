What is Project Proposal Writing?

A project is a task or scheme that requires a large amount of time, effort and planning to complete. To do a project one has to first prepare a project proposal and project proposal can be for various purposes ranging from construction to solving problems. The proposal attempts to get the reader to accept the writer's ideas.

Types of Proposals

External Proposal

External proposals are bids for a contract

Three Components:

  • Executive Summary - Overview of the proposal
  • Management Section - Project management details
  • Financial Section - Budget and cost analysis

Internal Proposal

Internal proposals are to win a co-worker or an administrator to accept an idea or enact a policy.

Requirements:

  • Planning the Proposal
  • Pay attention to audience
  • Collect information about the level of involvement
  • Get to know the knowledge of audience
  • Gather information on the level of authority
  • Use visuals

Writing the Proposal

The Introduction

  • Purpose - Clear statement of what you want to achieve
  • Credibility - Establish your expertise and qualifications
  • Definition of problem - Clearly define the issue to be addressed
  • Background of problem - Provide context and history
  • Significance of problem - Explain why this matters
  • Solution - Present your proposed approach
  • Preview of parts or sections - Outline the proposal structure

Discussion

  • The problem (in full detail) - Comprehensive analysis of the issue
  • The solution (to convince readers) - Detailed explanation of your approach
  • The context - Schedule for implementation, personnel involved, and the solution
