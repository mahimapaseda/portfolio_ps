01. Introduction to Professional Skills

Foundation module covering employability skills, workplace competencies, and the Johari Window framework for personal development.

Core Employability Skills

Communication

Convey information effectively through various channels including verbal, written, and non-verbal communication.

Teamwork

Work effectively with others and support team goals while maintaining positive relationships.

Problem-Solving

Analyze information and develop effective solutions to complex workplace challenges.

Adaptability

Embrace change and adjust to different situations and environments effectively.

Time Management

Organize and prioritize tasks to meet deadlines and achieve goals efficiently.

Leadership

Guide and motivate others towards common goals while inspiring team members.

The Johari Window Framework

Visual framework to understand conscious and unconscious biases

Self-Awareness

Understand your strengths and blind spots to improve personal effectiveness

Personal Development

Identify areas for improvement and growth in professional settings

Better Relationships

Build stronger workplace connections through improved understanding

Key Learning Outcomes

Self-Improvement

Understand what skills to learn for increased productivity and career advancement.

Continuous Learning

Seek opportunities to expand knowledge and skills through various learning methods.

Error Correction

Identify and correct personal and professional weaknesses systematically.
